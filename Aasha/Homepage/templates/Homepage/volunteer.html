Volunteer as a Listener

Since, we are a not-for-profit organization, we also rely on the user community to help us achieve our goals. This doesn't just mean that we you can help us with your donations, but you can become an active listener and help people recover.
For this you will need to take the course designed to train you to handle cases that we recieve on this site and pass a test.

About Aasha

Aasha was developed at the Hackathon event of IT Meet 2018 held at IT Park, Banepa in hopes of helping people with depression and give them hope towards living a better life without the constant suffering. At Aasha we believe that every life matters. And we believe that everyone has something to share. We listen. We understand. We help you smile.
